Services

At Drapes we specialize in beautiful hand sewn, lined and interlined curtains, bespoke blinds, headboards, pelmets and cushions.

We offer a customer-centric service. We have knowledge and experience working on both contemporary and traditional projects, taking pride in understanding our clients' needs. We sell our fabrics by the metre for curtains, blinds and soft furnishings. On each project we can offer a complete measuring and full fitting service.

All our curtains are made to measure by our professional seamstresses. Handmade to the highest standards.
Bespoke blinds are becoming more popular than ever, Drapes can provide a variety of styles and designs.
Drapes will make for you cushions, bed valances, headboards, tablecloths, beanbags and more.
To complement and emphasize your made to measure curtains, Drapes offers a vast range of tracks and poles.
Our accessories collection offers beautiful items that will compliment and enhance your home or commercial space.
