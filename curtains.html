Curtains

Our curtains are made-to-measure by our professional seamstresses, handmade to the highest standards.
At Drapes we have been making curtains for over 40 years and we still embrace and love the challenge of making our clients vision a reality.

We have over 20,000 fabric samples / books within our showroom, but do not worry we have an able team at hand to help guide & refine fabrics suitable for you.
In addition we are more than happy to visit you home or workplace to help achieve your vision.

We offer our clients a range of linings from Saten Chromax to full blackout grade three. Similarly our interlinings come in several weightings depending on choice of fabric and we are obviously happy to guide and advice you.
At Drapes we understand choosing fabric can be a daunting task with so many possibilities in both design and fabric textures available.

We offer a full measuring and fitting service.
