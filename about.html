About Drapes-Oxon

The Drapes-Oxon name has been linked with quality products, services and craftsmanship for nearly forty years. We pride ourselves on the quality of our workmanship and the care we take of our customers, both private and commercial.

We offer a bespoke and readymade service for blinds and curtains of all types. Choose from our extensive range of fabrics and we will do the rest – from your first consultation to the final fitting by our experienced craftsmen. We supply beautiful metal and wooden poles; tracks both modern and traditional, and wallpaper; paints and much more.

Our master upholsterers can also breathe new life into any item or we can help source furniture to create the look you desire.

We offer a full measuring and fitting service within Oxfordshire, and surrounding areas as well as London and have helped and worked for clients as far as Sydney. Then plus all the help and advice you require working within your budget.

Karen, Director and Co-Owner of Drapes-Oxon, brings passion and drive to every element of Drapes. With a wealth of business expertise Karen and her team will work alongside you to meet you requirements and much more.

Hilly Co-Owner and Director of Drapes-Oxon, oversees the marketing, advertising and financial side of the company. Working alongside Karen and the rest of the Drapes team to deliver you, our client the best service.

Bethany manages the day to day work within the showroom and workroom. Having graduated in Textile Design, she is able to offer a wealth of knowledge within the industry to our clients.
