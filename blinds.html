Blinds

Bespoke blinds are becoming more popular than ever with a variety of styles and designs to choose from.

Roman Binds with their crisp folds can offer a more modern and contemporary look and feel to a room. Additionally you may like to enhance your blind by adding borders, contrasting trims and beads.

Drapes can make and supply the following:

  • Roman blinds
  • Vertical blinds
  • Roller Blinds
  • Venetian blinds
